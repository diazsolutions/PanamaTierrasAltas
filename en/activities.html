Adventures in the Heights

Adventure

Hiking and Climbing

From gentle walks through forested trails to the challenging ascent of Baru Volcano. Panama offers routes for all fitness levels.

  • Quetzals Trail
  • Sleeping Indian
  • La Amistad International Park
Culture

Coffee Tours

Immerse yourself in the world of specialty coffee. Learn about cultivation, harvesting, and the roasting process of the world's most awarded coffee.

Boquete and Volcan are the heart of this industry, with farms offering professional tastings and educational tours.

Nature

Birdwatching

Panama is a birdwatcher's paradise. In the highlands, the Resplendent Quetzal is the star, but you'll also see toucans, hummingbirds, and hundreds of migratory species.
