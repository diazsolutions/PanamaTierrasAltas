Ecotourism

Connect with the richest biodiversity in the region through sustainable and environmentally friendly practices.

What does this activity include?

Flora and Fauna Observation

Discover endemic species and exotic orchids.

Guided Ecological Tours

Learn about ecosystems with local experts.

Visits to Natural Reserves

Explore protected areas dedicated to conservation.

Environmental Education

Participate in awareness and care programs.

Ecotourism in the highlands of Panama is much more than a simple visit; it's an immersion in one of the most important biodiversity hotspots in the world. From the cloud forests of Chiriqui to the reserves of Veraguas and Cocle, the region offers a sanctuary for thousands of species.

We promote responsible tourism that minimizes environmental impact and benefits local communities. By choosing ecological tours, you directly contribute to the preservation of these vital ecosystems for the country's natural balance.
