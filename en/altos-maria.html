Altos de Maria

Exclusivity and nature in the mountains.

A Mountain Refuge

Altos de Maria is a private mountain residential community, but its trails and waterfalls are a must-see attraction for those seeking luxury and nature. Located at an altitude of between 500 and 1,100 meters.

It offers spring-like weather all year round and stunning views of the Picacho de Laguna de San Carlos.

Activities

River Hiking

Walk along the Maria river through paved trails and hanging bridges.

Waterfalls

Visit crystal clear water falls ideal for cooling off.

Viewpoints

Observation points with views of the Pacific Ocean and surrounding mountains.
