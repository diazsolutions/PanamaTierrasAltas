Bambito

A dream valley between Volcan and Cerro Punta.

The alpine refuge of Chiriqui

Bambito is a narrow and deep valley that connects the highlands of Volcan with Cerro Punta. It is famous for its cool climate and landscapes that resemble the Swiss Alps. The Chiriqui Viejo River flows through the valley, providing the perfect environment for raising rainbow trout.

Main Activities

  • Trout fishing in local ponds
  • Hiking along the riverbank
  • Mountain birdwatching
  • Relaxation in mountain hotels

Relevant Data

Bambito is known for hosting some of the most iconic hotels in the region, offering a luxury experience in the middle of nature.
