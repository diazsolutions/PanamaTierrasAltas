Boquete

The valley of eternal spring and the best coffee in the world.

The epicenter of mountain tourism

Boquete is undoubtedly the most emblematic highland destination in Panama. Located in a fertile valley surrounded by mountains and crossed by the Caldera River, it offers a perfect combination of adventure, relaxation, and culture. It is world-famous for its specialty coffee production (including Geisha), its vibrant expatriate community, and its cool climate that invites constant exploration.

Main Activities

  • Specialty coffee tours
  • Hiking (Los Quetzales Trail, Pipeline)
  • Rafting on the Chiriqui Viejo River
  • Canopy and hanging bridges

Relevant Data

Boquete annually celebrates the Flower and Coffee Fair, one of the most colorful and visited events in Panama.
