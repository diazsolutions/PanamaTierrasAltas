Panama, Panama

Cerro Azul

The closest mountain escape to the capital.

Nature at a Step

Cerro Azul is a mountainous area located less than an hour from Panama City. It is part of the Chagres National Park and offers a respite from the tropical heat with temperatures that drop considerably at night.

It is the perfect place for bird watching, walking through cloud trails, and enjoying artificial lakes and natural waterfalls.

Activities

Hiking

Explore trails that delve into the primary forest of Chagres Park.

Birdwatching

A key point to observe hummingbirds and other mountain species near the city.

Relaxation

Enjoy the cool climate and the tranquility of mountain residential communities.
