El Valle de Anton

Nature and peace inside a volcanic crater.

About El Valle

El Valle de Anton is a unique destination in the world: a picturesque town situated in the crater of an extinct volcano. Located just 2 hours from Panama City, it offers a perfect escape with spring-like weather and amazing biodiversity.

It is known for its handicraft market, hot springs, and fascinating geological formations like the "Sleeping Indian" (India Dormida).

What to Do

Sleeping Indian Hike

An iconic hike offering panoramic views of the entire crater from the summit.

Chorro El Macho

An impressive 35-meter waterfall surrounded by tropical forest.

Hot Springs

Relax in volcanic mineral water pools known for their healing properties.

Sunday Market

Find local handicrafts, exotic flowers, and fresh fruits from the region.

Quick Facts

  • Climate 18°C - 26°C
  • Altitude 600 msl
