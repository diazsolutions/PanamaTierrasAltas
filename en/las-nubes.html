Las Nubes

Where the sky touches the earth at the highest point of Cerro Punta.

A corner of peace in the heights

Las Nubes is one of the highest and most picturesque areas of Cerro Punta, in the Chiriqui province. Its name is no coincidence; the mist usually descends to touch the fields, creating a magical and fresh atmosphere. It is home to the administrative headquarters of the La Amistad International Park (PILA) and offers spectacular views of the surrounding mountains. It is an ideal place to walk, enjoy the fresh air, and observe high mountain agricultural life.

Main Activities

  • Hiking through mountain trails
  • Visit to the Las Nubes viewpoint
  • High mountain bird watching
  • Landscape and agriculture photography

Relevant Data

From Las Nubes, several trails start that enter the La Amistad International Park.
