Palmira

Coffee landscapes and infinite views in the hills of Boquete.

The high-altitude coffee paradise

Palmira is one of the most beautiful and productive areas of the Boquete district. Its rolling hills are covered with coffee plantations that produce some of the best beans in the world. From its highest points, spectacular views of the Caldera River canyon and the imposing Volcan Baru can be appreciated.

Main Activities

  • Coffee tours in boutique farms
  • Hiking through scenic rural roads
  • Highland birdwatching
  • Mountain landscape photography

Relevant Data

Palmira is known for its tranquility and for being the home of several "Best of Panama" award-winning farms.
