Paso Ancho

The gateway to the majesty of Volcan Baru.

Nature in its purest state

Paso Ancho is a peaceful community located on the slopes of Volcan Baru. It is known for being one of the main access points to the National Park, offering trails that lead into the cloud forest. Its privileged location allows for stunning views of the volcano and an enviable climate throughout the year.

Main Activities

  • Ascent to Volcan Baru (Paso Ancho route)
  • Cloud forest hiking
  • Wildlife and flora observation
  • Adventure tourism and 4x4

Relevant Data

Paso Ancho is ideal for those seeking a more authentic and less commercial mountain experience than other nearby destinations.
