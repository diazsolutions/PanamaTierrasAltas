Potrerillos

Land of orange groves and the most imposing view of the volcano.

The citrus heart of Chiriqui

Potrerillos is an area divided into Potrerillos Arriba and Potrerillos Abajo, famous for its cool climate and its production of citrus, especially high-quality oranges. It is one of the places that offers the clearest and most majestic views of Volcan Baru, making it a favorite destination for photographers and nature lovers.

Main Activities

  • Visit to orange farms
  • Volcan Baru photography
  • Hiking through fresh rural trails
  • Birdwatching in forested areas

Relevant Data

Potrerillos Arriba enjoys a special microclimate that makes it significantly cooler than the surrounding lowlands.
