San Francisco

Colonial history and nature on the slopes of the mountain range.

A journey to the colonial past

San Francisco de la Montaña is a historic town in the Veraguas province, world-famous for its colonial church, a masterpiece of Churrigueresque baroque art. Situated in the foothills of the Central Mountain Range, it offers a cool climate and a natural environment full of rivers and hills that invite exploration.

Main Activities

  • Visit to the Church of San Francisco de la Montaña
  • Bathing in the San Francisco Waterfall
  • Hiking through the surrounding hills
  • Colonial architecture photography

Relevant Data

The San Francisco church is a National Historic Monument and contains exceptionally beautiful wood-carved altars.
