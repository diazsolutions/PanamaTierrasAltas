Travel Tips

What to Pack

Unlike the coast, the climate in the highlands is cool and it can rain at any time.

  • • Light jacket or windbreaker
  • • Hiking shoes with good grip
  • • Raincoat or umbrella
  • • Sunscreen (the sun is strong at altitude)
  • • Insect repellent

Transportation

The most common way to get there is by land from Panama City or by flying to David.

  • Car: The Inter-American Highway is in good condition. A 4x4 car is recommended if you plan to explore rural roads.
  • Bus: There are frequent buses from Albrook Terminal to David, and local buses from there to Boquete or Volcan.

Safety

The highlands are generally very safe, but it's always good to follow these recommendations:

  • • Do not go on difficult trails without a certified guide.
  • • Notify your hotel before leaving for a long hike.
  • • Stay hydrated and carry energy snacks.
