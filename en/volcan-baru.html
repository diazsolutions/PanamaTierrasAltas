Volcan Baru

The summit of Panama, where the sky meets two seas.

The ultimate challenge of the highlands

Volcan Baru is the highest point in Panama, with an altitude of 3,475 meters above sea level. It is a potentially active volcano located in the Chiriqui province. Climbing its summit is one of the most rewarding experiences for any traveler, as on clear days it is possible to see simultaneously the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea. The ascent can be done on foot through challenging trails or in specialized 4x4 vehicles.

Main Activities

  • Night hiking to see the sunrise
  • 4x4 vehicle tours to the summit
  • Camping in designated areas
  • High mountain flora and fauna observation

Relevant Data

The temperature at the summit can drop below 0°C, something unique in the Panamanian territory.
