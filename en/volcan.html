Volcan

Panama's "Little Switzerland," a hub for adventure and agrotourism.

The heart of the Highlands

Volcan is one of the most vibrant communities in the Chiriqui province. Located on the slopes of Volcan Baru, it offers a unique blend of modern services and pristine natural beauty. It is known for its cool climate, alpine-style architecture, and importance as a service center for the Highlands region. It is the ideal starting point for exploring lagoons, rivers, and farms producing flowers and vegetables.

Main Activities

  • Visit to the Volcan Hot Springs
  • Trout fishing in Bambito
  • Exploration of the Volcan Lagoons
  • Agrotourism and flower tours

Relevant Data

Volcan is famous for its "Sitio Barriles," an archaeological site that shows the pre-Columbian history of the region.
